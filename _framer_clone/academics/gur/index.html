Growing Up With Robotics

Overview

Growing Up With Robotics is an education-focused initiative aimed at expanding access to robotics, programming, and engineering for K–12 students. The project includes curriculum development, mentorship structures, workshops, seasonal camps, and team-to-team guidance programs that pair experienced robotics teams with newer ones.

This work centers on scaling impact—building systems that help others learn independently, not just delivering one-off events. It demonstrates leadership, organizational design, and a long-term commitment to growing technical communities rather than operating in isolation.
