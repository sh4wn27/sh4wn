I aim to make a real difference through the work I build and the communities I serve

Im a student driven by curiosity, responsibility, and a strong connection to the communities that shaped me. My background has pushed me to focus not just on learning, but on building, creating systems, tools, and opportunities that give back in meaningful ways. Serving others isnt something I see as optional; its central to how I define growth, leadership, and impact.

Over the years, Ive volunteered with nonprofit organizations such as Area Youth Ministry and Growing Up with Robotics, where I worked directly with younger students through mentorship and STEM education. Ive pursued research experiences with universities and institutions to deepen my technical thinking, and Ive gained real-world exposure by interning at a VC-backed startup, Venture Camp, where I saw how ideas turn into scalable impact. I believe in developing across disciplines such as engineering, research, entrepreneurship, and service is crucial to build a well-rounded, dependable character and to be someone others can trust to execute, lead, and contribute wherever I am needed.
